Hi! I'm Julio

I'm a developer and artist who blends advanced technical expertise with creative expression. As a Full-Stack Engineer and AI Developer, I build intelligent, scalable applications using Python/Django, React, and Node.js, backed by Postgres, MySql and MongoDB, and deployed on production AWS infrastructure including ECS, Lambda, RDS, and CloudFront. My work spans the full data pipeline architecting ETL flows that consolidate data across advertising platforms, affiliate networks, and internal systems, and developing machine learning models for lead scoring, conversion attribution, and audience segmentation. I design automation workflows with n8n and AWS Lambda, craft prompts for cutting edge LLMs, and build AI agents that turn complex operations into seamless experiences.

Alongside my engineering work, I bring a deep artistic vision sculpting characters in ZBrush, modeling and rigging in Blender, and producing illustration and comic work through Photoshop and Procreate. Whether I'm shipping AI-powered platforms or telling stories through color and form, I'm driven by a passion to push boundaries and deliver work that's both functional and inspiring. I thrive at the intersection of technology, creativity and designing solutions that are efficient, emotionally resonant, and user-centric.
